Biryani Recipe

Biryani is a flavorful rice dish made by cooking rice with meat or vegetables, spices, and aromatics. The ingredients are layered and cooked slowly so the rice absorbs the rich flavors. Biryani is known for its strong aroma, colorful appearance, and delicious taste, and it is often served on special occasions.

Ingredients

  • Basmati rice
  • Meat
  • Onions
  • Garlic
  • Yoghurt
  • Ghee
  • Tomatoes
  • Biryani spices
  • Salt
  • Fresh corriander and mint
  • Water

Steps

  1. Wash the rice and set it aside to drain.
  2. Heat oil or ghee in a pot and fry the onions until golden brown.
  3. Add garlic and ginger and cook briefly.
  4. Add meat or vegetables and fry until partially cooked.
  5. Add tomatoes , yogurt, biryani spices, and salt. Cook until the mixture is thick and flavorful.
  6. In a separate pot, boil water, add a little salt, and cook the rice until it is half done. Drain
  7. Layer the partially cooked rice over the meat or vegetable mixture. Sprinkle fresh coriander and mint if available.
  8. Cover the pot and cook on low heat (dum cooking) until the rice is fully cooked and the flavors are absorbed.
  9. Gently mix before serving and serve hot.
